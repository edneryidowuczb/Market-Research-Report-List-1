<p><p>Integrated Facility Management (IFM) encompasses various market types, primarily categorized as On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise solutions involve software installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant IT resources and maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based IFM solutions are hosted on remote servers, enabling easy access, scalability, and reduced infrastructure costs. This model allows businesses to leverage technology without extensive hardware investments, fostering collaboration and real-time data management over the internet.</p></p>
