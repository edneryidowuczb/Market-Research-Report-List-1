<p><ul><li>PCR</li><li>PCR-RFLP</li><li>AS-PCR or PCR-SSP</li><li>Multiplex PCR</li><li>Real Time PCR</li><li>Sanger DNA Sequencing</li><li>Pyrosequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The molecular blood market encompasses several key technologies for DNA analysis and diagnostics. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies DNA, while PCR-RFLP (Restriction Fragment Length Polymorphism) analyzes sequence variations. AS-PCR (Allele-Specific PCR) or PCR-SSP (Sequence-Specific Priming) detects specific alleles. Multiplex PCR allows simultaneous amplification of multiple targets. Real-Time PCR provides quantitative data during amplification. Sanger DNA Sequencing offers high accuracy for short sequences, while Pyrosequencing enables rapid, real-time analysis of DNA sequences, enhancing diagnostic capabilities in various applications.</p></p>
