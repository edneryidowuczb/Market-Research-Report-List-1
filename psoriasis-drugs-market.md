<p><strong>The Psoriasis Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tumor Necrosis Factor Inhibitor</li><li>Interleukin Inhibitors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The psoriasis drugs market is categorized into three main types: Tumor Necrosis Factor (TNF) inhibitors, Interleukin inhibitors, and Others. TNF inhibitors work by blocking the action of proteins that promote inflammation, thus reducing psoriatic symptoms. Interleukin inhibitors target specific interleukins involved in the inflammatory process, offering a more targeted approach to treatment. The "Others" category includes various therapies, such as topical treatments and systemic agents, providing alternative options for patients and enhancing overall treatment strategies in managing psoriasis.</p></p>
