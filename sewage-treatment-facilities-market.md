<p><p>Sewage treatment facilities utilize various advanced technologies to enhance wastewater management. Moving Bed Biofilm Reactors (MBBR) leverage biofilm growth on moving media for efficient organic matter degradation. Ozone-Based Advanced Oxidation employs ozone to oxidize contaminants, effectively reducing pathogens and organic pollutants. Powdered Activated Carbon (PAC) enhances adsorption processes by removing dissolved organic substances and improving effluent quality. Together, these technologies improve treatment efficiency, reduce environmental impact, and enable compliance with stringent regulatory standards in wastewater management.</p></p>
