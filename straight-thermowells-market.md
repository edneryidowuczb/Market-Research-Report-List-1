<p><p>Straight thermowells are cylindrical devices designed to protect temperature sensors, such as thermocouples and RTDs, while allowing accurate temperature readings in various industrial processes. These components are commonly used in applications where direct immersion of temperature sensors is impractical or where conditions may be harmful to the sensor. Their primary function is to provide a barrier between the sensor and the process fluid, ensuring the longevity and reliability of the measurement.</p><p>The Straight Thermowells Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.5% during the forecast period.
